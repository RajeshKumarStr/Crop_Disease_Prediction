Crop Disease Prediction System

Our Crop Disease Prediction System is an advanced AI-powered solution designed to help farmers and agricultural experts detect diseases in crops with high accuracy. Using cutting-edge machine learning and deep learning algorithms, the system analyzes images of crop leaves to identify potential diseases early, allowing for timely intervention and improved crop health.

How It Works

Simply upload an image of a crop leaf, and our model will predict the disease, providing valuable insights to prevent further damage. This tool aims to enhance precision farming and contribute to a more sustainable agricultural future.
